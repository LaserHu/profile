About Me


Hu Changshuo is currently a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Singapore Management University, under the supervision of Professor Ma Dong. His research interests encompass mobile computing and pervasive sensing, with a particular focus on authentication, running monitoring, and interaction using earables.
